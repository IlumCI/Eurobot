Cookie Policy

Effective 21 July 2026

This policy explains the cookies and similar local storage that valtgeist.trade uses, and how you control them. We keep this deliberately minimal — the same engineering-first stance as the rest of the site.

Plain summary: today this site sets no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. It uses only a little local storage to make the site work (your theme and your consent choice), and our traffic measurement (Cloudflare) is cookieless. Anything non-essential we might add in future loads only after you opt in.

§01What are cookies and local storage

Cookies are small files a site can store in your browser; "local storage" is a similar browser mechanism for saving small preferences on your device. Both let a site remember things between visits. Some are strictly necessary for the site to function; others are optional.

§03Analytics

We measure basic, aggregate traffic (page views, referrers, rough geography) using Cloudflare's privacy-first web analytics, which does not set cookies and does not fingerprint or track individuals across sites. Because it is cookieless, it needs no consent — but it is still disclosed here for transparency.

§04If we add optional cookies later

If we ever introduce cookies that are not strictly necessary (for example, a cookie-based analytics or product tool), we will list them here and load them only after you opt in via the consent banner. Rejecting them will always be as easy as accepting them.

§05Managing your choices

You can change your consent at any time using the button below, which reopens the consent banner. You can also clear or block cookies and local storage in your browser settings; note that clearing storage will reset your theme and consent preferences, so we may ask again.

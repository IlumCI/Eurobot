## Value props (in priority order)

1. **Non-custodial — your keys, your vault.** Funds sit in a vault only you can
   withdraw from. The pod holds an operator key that can *only* rebalance on
   whitelisted pools; it can never withdraw. (Post-FTX, this is the whole ballgame.)
2. **Flat fee, not a cut.** You pay a subscription, not a percentage of your capital
   or profits. We don't earn more when you take more risk — incentives aligned.
3. **Real risk management, not a yield meme.** Cascade detector, pump-mint veto,
   asymmetric protective bands, panic-flatten, equity floor. It's engineered to
   survive, not to post a screenshot.
4. **Transparent.** Live telemetry — state, position, price, P&L, fees, risk signals
   — streamed to your dashboard. No black box.
5. **Research-backed.** Built by Euroswarms; the mechanics are real and documented.

## Differentiators (use when someone says "how is this different from…")

- **…LPing myself?** You won't sit there 24/7 detecting cascades and rebalancing.
  It does — with discipline you can't maintain manually, and guards that stop you
  buying the pump.fun trap.
- **…a yield farm / vault protocol?** Those usually pool everyone's money and/or
  take custody. We don't pool and we don't custody — one pod, one wallet, yours.
- **…a CeFi bot / managed fund?** They hold your funds. We can't touch yours. If we
  vanish tomorrow, your money is still in your vault.
- **…other trading bots?** Most are naked strategies. Phoenix is a strategy wrapped
  in a *survival* system — the guards and the floor are the point.

## Objection handling (keep these answers ready)

- **"Is this a scam / another rug?"**
  > Fair question — most things are. That's exactly why it's non-custodial: your
  > funds stay in a vault only you can withdraw from, we literally cannot take them.
  > And we're open about risk — market-making can lose money; the whole design is
  > about not blowing up.

- **"So what returns do I get?"**
  > We don't promise returns and we never will — anyone who does is lying to you. We
  > promise disciplined execution and full transparency. You can lose money
  > market-making; our job is to manage that risk, not to guarantee an outcome.

- **"Non-custodial *how*, exactly?"**
  > Your capital lives in an on-chain vault you own. The pod is granted a narrow
  > operator permission: it can rebalance within whitelisted pools, nothing else.
  > Withdrawal is owner-only — always you. (Live at launch; the vault is being
  > audited first, which is why we're pre-launch.)

- **"Why should I trust the strategy?"**
  > Don't trust it — inspect it. It's built on documented mechanics from Euroswarms
  > research, and every pod streams live telemetry so you see exactly what it's
  > doing. Start small.

- **"When can I actually use it?"**
  > Pre-launch now. Real funds go live once the vault is audited — we won't cut that
  > corner. Join the waitlist and you'll be first in.

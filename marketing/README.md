# Vältgeist — marketing kit

You're an engineer, not a marketer. That's fine — this kit is written so you can
open it cold tomorrow and *use* it, not study it. Everything here is paste-ready or
close to it.

## The one thing to internalise first

**We are pre-launch.** The product cannot take real funds yet (the vault must be
deployed and audited first). So the *entire* goal of marketing right now is:

> Build an audience and grow the waitlist. Nothing else.

That means **never** imply people can deposit, earn, or get returns *today*. Not
because it's illegal to be excited — but because over-promising a financial product
is how you get in trouble and lose trust. The honest pre-launch story is genuinely
*stronger* than hype anyway: "here's the engineering, here's what we're building,
here's why it's non-custodial, join the list." See `compliance-guardrails.md`
before you post anything — it's short and it will save you.

## Our angle (why we win)

Crypto marketing is 99% hype and half of it is scams. Our edge is being the
opposite: **the boring, honest, non-custodial, engineering-first one.** Lead with
substance. The spec-sheet aesthetic on the site is the brand — carry that voice
everywhere. Anti-hype *is* the hype.

## If you only do 3 things tomorrow

1. **Claim the handles.** Create the X/Twitter account (and grab the name on
   Farcaster + Reddit). Use the bio in `copy-bank.md`. Pin the launch post.
2. **Post the launch thread** from `copy-bank.md` (X). Cross-post the short version
   to r/solana-adjacent places and any Solana LP Discord you're in.
3. **Email the waitlist** (even if it's 3 people) with the update template in
   `copy-bank.md`. Momentum compounds; a list you never talk to dies.

Everything else is optional polish.

## A simple first-week sequence (don't overthink it)

- **Day 1** — Handles + launch thread + waitlist email. (The 3 things above.)
- **Day 2** — Reply to comments. Post one "build-in-build-public" tweet: what you
  shipped (the live paper-mode pod telemetry demo is a *great* first flex — a real
  strategy trading a real pool with demo money, streamed live).
- **Day 3** — Write one deeper post on a single differentiator (the cascade
  detector, or "why non-custodial actually matters"). Depth earns follows.
- **Day 4–5** — Engage in others' threads (Solana LP / DeFi). Don't pitch; be
  useful. Drop the link only when it's genuinely relevant.
- **Weekend** — Recap post. Numbers if you have them ("X on the waitlist"),
  otherwise a "what's next" teaser (the vault + first live pods).

Repeat. Marketing pre-launch is just: show the work, tell the truth, invite people,
repeat. You don't need to be clever. You need to be consistent and credible — which,
as the engineer who actually built this, you already are.
